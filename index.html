A Propos De Nous

Some think that we will not change the world... We will! And with the most daily act in the world: eating it! Simple and healthy cuisine 100% organic supply. Here are the assets of Delice to create a different world, more human, more respectful, more harmonious. Behind each of our dishes, there are kitchens that carve, cut, cook, season, suppliers and passionate farmers, involved in preserving biodiversity. Thus, by feasting, you perform an act of benevolence for yourself, for others and for the planet. Just that!
